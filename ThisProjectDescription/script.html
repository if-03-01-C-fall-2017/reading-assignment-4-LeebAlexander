Insight

When people try to enter the world of social media the often get to know a platorm through friends. But with this project we are trying to give the person an objective view on what social media platform would be optimal for him. We want to achieve this goal through good placed and formulated questions that tell nough about the person to make an accurate prediction. Our project tries to target everyone who is uncertain which social media he or she should use. So we help him out through our website. We plan on extending the site with other tools or quiz that target social media aswell.
